About

Modint Intelligence

An emotion AI company built on the thesis that affect is the missing infrastructure layer in artificial intelligence.

The thesis

Current AI treats emotion as annotation — a label slapped on after the fact. HEART treats emotion as architecture. It's a continuous, multi-dimensional affect engine baked into the reasoning process itself.

Modint Intelligence is a one-person-company building four products that prove this thesis across domains: personal AI, clinical mental health, financial intelligence, and education. Each product is a proof-of-concept for the HEART engine, which is being positioned as a standalone API platform.

Founded by Manvendra Modgil. Currently pursuing dual degrees in Data Science & AI (BITS Pilani) and Digital Business & Entrepreneurship (IIM Bangalore).
